Our Story

Fashion Without Borders

Blessing Collection was born from a simple belief: that women in Addis Ababa deserve access to the same quality and style as any fashion capital in the world.

We source directly from Turkey's premier fashion houses and cosmetic manufacturers, cutting out the middleman so our clients receive authentic, high-quality pieces at fair prices. From elegant evening dresses to everyday essentials, luxury cosmetics to fine jewellery — everything is hand-selected with the Ethiopian woman in mind.

Visit us at Yeka View Apartment, or place a custom order and we will source exactly what you are looking for.

  • Directly imported from Turkey — no middlemen
  • Authentic, high-quality pieces guaranteed
  • Custom orders sourced on request
  • Personal styling consultations available
